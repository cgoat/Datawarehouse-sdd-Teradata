# Mission

## One-line pitch

A datawarehouse that will ingest multiple csv files and provide a reporting layer for consumption.

## Why it exists

Ingest and Scale large files in the , clean the files per the logic in a staging area, integrate various datasets in the consumption area. 

A datawarehouse is efficient in parsing , transforming and integerating data in scale.


## Target audience

**Business Analysts** who want to develop visualiztion reports to do analytics and make business decisions.

## Goals

- **Timely** Provide data for timely decision making
- **Quality** Data that adheres to the business rules

## Non-goals

-  SaaS infrastructure
-  Reporting tool

## Personas

- **Business Analyst** — Analyses data in the consumption layer and develops  reports
- **CEO** — Reviews the data provided by the Business analyst
- **Reliability Engineer** - Monitors the pipelines for any failures during ingestion, transformation and integeration
